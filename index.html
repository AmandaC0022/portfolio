Little Bit About Me:

Hi! My name is Amanda Morgan and I am currently an Elementary School Art Teacher in Columbiana, Alabama. I am working on becoming a Website Developer.

Fun Fact about me is I love to Crochet. My Granny taught me when I was 8 years old and I have been crocheting ever since. I own an Etsy Shop called, The Retro Stitch , where I sell handmade stuffed animals. I hand draw each animal and then write a pattern. I teach Crocheting classes at our local Arts Council.

I am also a huge Auburn fan. My family has been attending Auburn college since it was an all male's college in the late 1800's. I also played Clarinet in the Auburn Marching Band. I performed at the 2010 National Championship when we won against the Oregon Ducks. I own the Women's Version of the Championship ring.
